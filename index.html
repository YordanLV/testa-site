About

-------

TESTA AUTOTRASPORTI was born in 2008, after the takeover of the father Paride, proposes logistics solutions "tailor-made" in order to meet the demands of customers, carrying in road mode, train and naval, with maximum efficiency and safety a wide range of chemical products, as well as liquid industrial waste also classified as hazardous.

A continuous path of progress has always been the sign of the innovation of the processes . Testa is highly vocated to the Internationalisation and thanks to a high level of quality service , our company is a guarantee in the chemical transport world.

Our business concept is based on flexibility and innovation. These factors guarantee the continuity of our company.

That’s why we concentrate on doing whatever is possible – being a modern logistics.

Our Fleet

-------

The road tractor fleet is monitored by a satellite control system, integrated with a management information system to provide customers with an update in real-time of the status of their shipments.

Furthermore, our GPS system is integrated with our management software; in this way, it is possible both the constant monitoring and control of the movements of the vehicles, and the monitoring of the routes with possibility of intervention of emergencies 24 hours.

Our company fleet is about 50 tractors , which are changed every 3-5 years , in order to guarantee high quality level of services.The fleet of road tankers respond with flexibility to market needs by adapting to the Transport of different products, from solvents, additives for cement to highly viscous products, thanks to air compressors, flow pumps and flowmeters.
